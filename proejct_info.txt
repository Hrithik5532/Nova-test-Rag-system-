Company Background: NOVA
NOVA is a fast-growing D2C fashion and beauty brand founded in 2019. It sells skincare, makeup,
apparel, and accessories directly to consumers through its website and mobile app.
• 2.1 million registered customers across 14 countries
• 85,000+ SKUs across 6 product categories — skincare, makeup, hair, apparel, footwear,
accessories
• A customer support team of 120 agents handling ~18,000 tickets per week
• Average order value of $74 and customer lifetime value of $380
• Net Promoter Score of 42, below the industry benchmark of 58
• Support ticket backlog growing 23% month-over-month due to scaling pains.
Business Problem
NOVA's CEO has set a bold target: reduce support costs by 40% and increase repeat purchase rate
by 25% within 12 months, using AI. You have been hired as an AI Engineer to build the platform from
scratch.

📣 Brief from NOVA's VP of Engineering
"We're drowning in support tickets. 60% of them are the same five questions — order status,
returns, product recommendations, sizing, and ingredient queries. Our support agents spend
80% of their time on these, leaving no bandwidth for complex cases that actually need humans."
"On top of that, our recommendation engine is a rule-based disaster from 2021. It shows
customers products they already bought. We're leaving millions on the table."
"I need an AI platform that handles routine queries end-to-end, surfaces the right product
to the right customer at the right moment, and hands off to a human seamlessly when it
can't cope. And it must explain every decision — our legal team requires full audit trails
for any AI-generated customer communication."

Business Context
This is the capstone, bringing all four (1-4) tasks together. NOVA's AI Support Platform is a
LangGraph multi-agent system that routes tickets, uses RAG to ground answers (Task 3), calls
backend tools via MCP (Task 2), generates responses in NOVA brand voice via the fine-tuned model
(Task 4), applies the prompt logic from Task 1, and hands off to humans when needed, all with a full
audit trail for legal compliance.

Google Colab Free Tier
All tasks must run on Google Colab Free. No paid compute is required. The entire
assessment is designed to fit within these constraints.

Data & Assets
No manual dataset creation is required. All data is sourced from free public datasets or
auto-generated by the LLM itself. E.g Kaggle, Python Faker.
