PRODUCT: HydraGlow Vitamin C Serum
SKU: SKU-SC-001
Category: Skincare
Price: $38.00
Rating: 4.7/5

Description:
A brightening serum with 15% Vitamin C, Hyaluronic Acid, and Niacinamide.
Targets dark spots, uneven skin tone, and signs of aging. Lightweight,
fast-absorbing formula suitable for daily use under sunscreen.

Key Ingredients:
- Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid) 15%: Powerful antioxidant, brightens skin,
  boosts collagen production, reduces hyperpigmentation.
- Hyaluronic Acid: Attracts and retains moisture, plumps fine lines.
- Niacinamide (Vitamin B3) 5%: Minimizes pores, strengthens skin barrier,
  reduces redness. Note: Safe to use alongside Vitamin C in this formulation.
- Ferulic Acid 0.5%: Stabilizes Vitamin C, doubles its UV protection efficacy.
- Vitamin E: Moisturizes and protects against free radical damage.

Suitable Skin Types: Oily, Combination, Normal
NOT recommended for: Very sensitive or rosacea-prone skin (Vitamin C may cause
                       initial tingling — patch test first)

How to Use:
1. Cleanse face and pat dry
2. Apply 3–4 drops to face and neck
3. Wait 1 minute for absorption
4. Follow with moisturizer and SPF 30+
5. Use morning routine only (Vitamin C + Sunscreen = best results)

Frequently Asked Questions:
Q: Can I use this with retinol?
A: Yes, but alternate — Vitamin C in the morning, retinol at night.

Q: Will this stain my clothes?
A: The serum is clear and non-staining. Allow it to absorb before dressing.

Q: How long until I see results?
A: Most customers notice brighter skin in 2–3 weeks. Significant dark spot
   improvement typically takes 6–8 weeks of consistent use.
